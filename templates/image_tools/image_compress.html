❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 Can I compress an image to an exact file size (e.g., 500 KB)?
Yes! Our tool uses binary search to adjust quality and hits your target size very accurately.
🔹 Is it completely free?
Yes, 100% free with unlimited compressions. No signup, no watermarks.
🔹 Does it upload my image to a server?
No! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files stay private on your device.
🔹 Which image formats are supported?
JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, and SVG. For GIF animations, only the first frame is compressed. For SVG, it is rendered as a PNG then compressed.
🔹 What is the maximum file size allowed?
5MB – perfect for fast, efficient compression.
🔹 How close can you get to my target size?
Typically within 5% of your target. For best results, use high‑resolution images.
🔹 How does quality change with target size?
The tool automatically finds the best quality to meet your exact target size – smaller target means more quality reduction, larger target preserves more quality.

💡 Expert Tips for Best Results

For most accurate target size compression, use high‑resolution images. Our binary search algorithm quickly finds optimal quality. For SVG files, they are rasterized first; for GIFs, only the first frame is used.
