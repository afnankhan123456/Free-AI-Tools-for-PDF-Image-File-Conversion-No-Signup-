🔹 Can I convert any PDF to Word?
Yes, text‑based PDFs work perfectly. Scanned/image‑only PDFs may require OCR (coming in future updates).
🔹 Is it completely free?
Yes, 100% free with unlimited conversions. No signup, no watermarks.
🔹 Does it upload my PDF to a server?
No! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files stay private.
🔹 Why no external library?
To avoid CDN loading failures. We generate a .doc file using HTML – completely reliable and works in all modern Word processors.
🔹 What is the maximum PDF size?
