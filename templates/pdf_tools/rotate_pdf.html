❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 Can I rotate PDF pages by any angle?
Yes, you can use 90° clockwise/counter‑clockwise, 180°, or enter any custom angle (0–360°).
🔹 Is it completely free?
Yes, 100% free with unlimited rotations. No signup, no watermarks.
🔹 Does it upload my PDF to a server?
No! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files stay private.
🔹 Will the page size change after rotation?
For 90° and 270°, width and height swap to fit the rotated content. For 180°, dimensions remain the same.
🔹 What is the maximum PDF size?
Up to 5MB, ensuring fast client-side processing.
🔹 Does it preserve text and images quality?
Yes, we render at 2x scale and embed high‑quality JPEG images, so text remains sharp.
🔹 Can I rotate only specific pages?
Currently, the tool rotates all pages uniformly. For page‑specific rotation, try our advanced tool (coming soon).

💡 Expert Tips for Best Results

For scanned PDFs with wrong orientation, use 90° presets. For custom angles like 15° or 45°, the output will be rotated but may have white borders. We automatically adjust page dimensions to fit the rotated content.

  • 🎯 Max file size 5MB ensures smooth client-side processing.

  • 🖼️ High quality – 2x scaling keeps text and images crisp.
  • ⚡ Custom angles – use any integer between 0 and 360.
