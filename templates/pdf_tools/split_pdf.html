🔹 Can I extract multiple non‑contiguous pages?
Yes, use commas: 1,3,5. For ranges, use hyphen: 1-3,5-7.
🔹 How to split every page of a PDF?
Click the "All Pages" button – it automatically fills the range with all page numbers.
🔹 Is it completely free?
Yes, 100% free with unlimited splits. No signup, no watermarks.
🔹 Does it upload my PDF to a server?
No! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files stay private.
🔹 What if I choose an out‑of‑range page?
The tool will ignore invalid pages and only extract valid ones.
🔹 What is the maximum PDF size?
Up to 5MB, ensuring fast client-side processing.
🔹 Does it preserve text and images quality?
Yes, we render at 2x scale and embed high‑quality JPEG images.

💡 Expert Tips for Best Results

For large PDFs, splitting all pages will generate many files. The ZIP download will contain each page as a separate PDF named "page_1.pdf", "page_2.pdf", etc. Use the "All Pages" button for quick split, or specify complex ranges like "1-3,5,7-9".

  • 📄 Range syntax – e.g., "1-3" extracts pages 1,2,3; "1,4,6" extracts pages 1,4,6.
